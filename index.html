Some Tips:


  • Always Try to evolve pokemon which are extra-large (you can tell when a pokemon is extra large xasue it will have XL as a superscript to it's weight and height category). Pokemon with the XL tag are almost always in the higher range of combat points after evolution.

  • Don't evolve pokemon with the XS tag unless you really really really love them.

  • Normal pokemon without the XS or XL tag can go either way. Depends on your luck.
